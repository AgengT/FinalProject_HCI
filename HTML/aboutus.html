WatcHGoodS is a store that focused in selling luxury and casual watches, WatcHGoodS was founded in 1984 by two person Christopher and Samuel, they started selling watches by opening a store in their hometown New York City, after a couple of successful year they started branching their stores in outside the country specifically in London, since then they have open yet another 3 more branch in Tokyo, Jakarta, and Berlin while still maintaining the first store in NY City.
